Miso Soup Recipe

Description

No Japaense meal is complete without soup. Soups are staple dishes that rounds out nearly every home cooked meal, and miso soup is one of the most popular.

While seeminly simple, making a delicious miso soup at home requires patience and balance. A good dashi is the foundation of miso soup and it important to create a flavorful dashi off which to build.

Ingredients

  • Granulated dashi
  • Water
  • Miso
  • Spring onion
  • Dried wakame seaweed

Steps

  1. Place a handful of dried wakame seaweed into a bowl and add cold water. Make sure the bowl is had extra space because the dried seaweed will expand as it hydrates
  2. Finely slice the spring onion into rounds
  3. Add water to sauce pan along with 2 spoons of dashi powder. Cook over medium-high heat until boiling
  4. Once boiling, turn off burner and stir in 1 heaping spoonful of miso and let soup stand.
  5. Grab a handful to hydrated wakame seaweed and squeeze out excess water. Place into soup bowl.
  6. Ladle soup into soup bowl over wakame.
  7. Add pinch of sliced spring onion.
  8. Enjoy piping hot.
